THE 6 DAY DEPLOYMENT

Your strategic operating plan balancing culture, coffee, and exploration.

DAY 1: ARRIVAL & ACCLIMATIZATION (Sun, 28 Jun)

  • 07:00 AM: Arrive in Dharamshala/McLeod Ganj via HRTC Bus. Take a shared cab/auto up to the main square.
  • 09:00 AM: Breakfast & First Coffee at Shiva Cafe.
  • 01:00 PM: Check in at goSTOPS Mcleodganj. Drop the Safari bag. Deploy Decathlon rig.
  • 03:00 PM: Light exploration of the Main Square. Late lunch at Tibet Kitchen.
  • 06:00 PM: Walk down to Illiterati Books & Coffee. Watch the sunset, drink coffee, and read Camus.

DAY 2: TIBETAN CULTURE & HISTORY (Mon, 29 Jun)

  • 09:00 AM: Morning pour over at the hostel from your Blue Tokai stash.
  • 10:30 AM: Visit the Dalai Lama Temple Complex (Tsuglagkhang).
  • 12:30 PM: Explore the Tibet Museum.
  • 02:00 PM: Lunch at Snow Lion Restaurant.
  • 04:00 PM: Visit the Library of Tibetan Works and Archives.
  • 07:00 PM: Dinner at Jimmy's Italian Kitchen.

DAY 3: NATURE & ELEVATION (Tue, 30 Jun)

  • 08:00 AM: Trek to Bhagsu Waterfall early.
  • 11:00 AM: Continue upward towards Gallu Devi Temple.
  • 02:00 PM: Return to town. Stop at Woeser Bakery.
  • 05:00 PM: Rest at hostel, listen to the Sennheiser IE 200 stack.

DAY 4: OBSERVATION & CAFE HOPPING (Wed, 01 Jul)

  • 10:00 AM: Take a cab or hike to Naddi View Point.
  • 01:00 PM: Head to Dharamkot. Have lunch at Morgan's Place.
  • 03:30 PM: Settle in at Crepe Pancake Hut.
  • 07:00 PM: Return to Mcleod Ganj. Street food or budget dinner near main square.

DAY 5: THE BUFFER DAY / UNEXPECTED FINDS (Thu, 02 Jul)

  • Open Morning: Visit Norbulingka Institute.
  • Lunch: Eat at the Norbulingka cafe.
  • Afternoon: Visit the St. John in the Wilderness Church.

DAY 6: EXTRACTION & SOUVENIRS (Fri & Sat, 03-04 Jul)

  • 03 Jul / 04 Jul Morning: Utilize your ₹2,000 Souvenir budget on Temple Road.
  • 04 Jul 10:00 AM: Check out of goSTOPS. Request them to hold your Safari bag.
  • 12:00 PM: Final lunch at Kunga Guest House / Nick's Italian Kitchen.
  • 05:30 PM: Retrieve Safari Bag. Repack to deployment standards.
  • 06:45 PM: Proceed to Mcleodganj Main Square for the cab pickup.
  • 07:15 PM: Extraction. FlixBus departs for Delhi.
